Edsa rehab phase 1 set for completion in July

BY: Mary Joy Salcedo

February 16, 2026

MANILA, Philippines — Phase 1 of the rehabilitation of Edsa, Metro Manila’s busiest thoroughfare, is targeted for completion in July, Public Works Secretary Vince Dizon said on Monday.

In a press conference, Dizon said Phase 1, covering Roxas Boulevard to Orense, is already nearly 50 percent complete.

“Phase 1, including sidewalks, will be completed by July 2026,” Dizon said.

He added that the section without sidewalks is expected to be finished in May.

“Yung mga dinadaanan ng kotse, yung carriageway na tinatawag nila, Mayo, tapos na yan for Phase 1,” the DPWH chief said.

(The roads where cars pass through, the carriageway, as they call it, that’s done by May, for Phase 1.)

Dizon also noted that the agency is already in the final stages of the preparation for the procurement of the project’s Phase 2, which he said will cover Orense to Cubao.

The Edsa rehabilitation began on December 24, 2025, with all reblocking work and asphalt overlay of busway lanes conducted 24 hours a day until 4 a.m. on January 5, 2026.

After January 5, road work is scheduled from 10 p.m. to 4 a.m.

From Monday to Friday, asphalt overlay is conducted lane by lane for both northbound and southbound traffic, while from Friday to Sunday, asphalt overlay and reblocking are carried out, one lane per direction. This schedule will continue until May 31, 2026.
